System Overview
Three primary flows covering the full lifecycle of a quiz — from creation to analysis.

Quiz Creator

Teacher or admin who designs, configures, and publishes quizzes. Manages question bank, sets rules, and reviews aggregate performance.

System Architecture — High Level Flow
Quiz Creator Flow
Step-by-step path a teacher or admin follows to build, configure, and publish a quiz.
Screen / Page
Decision Point
Start
End
① LOGIN / DASHBOARD
Creator logs in and sees their quiz library with draft, active, and archived quizzes.
② CREATE NEW QUIZ
Clicks "New Quiz" — can start from scratch or pick a template from the library.
③ QUIZ SETUP
Sets title, description, category, time limit, passing score, and randomization options.
④ ADD QUESTIONS
Adds MCQ, True/False, or Short Answer questions. Can reorder, duplicate, or delete. Loop until satisfied.
⑤ PREVIEW QUIZ
Takes the quiz as a taker would see it. Decision: satisfied → publish, or go back to edit.
⑥ CONFIGURE SHARING
Sets attempt limit, start/end date, password protection, and public vs invite-only access.
⑦ PUBLISH & SHARE
Gets shareable link, embed code, or QR code. Can share directly via email to a group.
⑧ MONITOR RESPONSES
Real-time view of submissions, completion rate, and live attempt count.
⑨ ANALYTICS & REPORTS
Score distribution, question-level analysis, export to CSV/PDF, and comparative performance.
Quiz Taker Flow
The path a student or participant follows from receiving a quiz link to submitting their answers.
Screen / Page
Decision Point
Start
End
① RECEIVE LINK
Taker gets a link via email, message, or QR code. May need to log in if the quiz is restricted.
② QUIZ INTRO SCREEN
Sees quiz title, description, number of questions, time limit, and passing score before starting.
③ ATTEMPT QUESTIONS
Answers questions one at a time or all on one page. Can navigate forward/back and flag questions to revisit.
④ REVIEW BEFORE SUBMIT
Summary page shows answered vs unanswered and flagged questions before final submission.
⑤ SUBMIT & AUTO-GRADE
On submit (or timer expiry), answers are automatically graded and the taker proceeds to the Results flow.
EDGE CASES
Timer expiry auto-submits. Password-protected quizzes require a code. Attempt limits are enforced before starting.
Results & Review Flow
Post-submission experience for the taker — plus the analytics view for the creator.
① SCORE SUMMARY
Shows total score, percentage, pass/fail status, and time taken. Animated score reveal for engagement.
② CERTIFICATE
Auto-generated on pass. Taker can download as PDF or share to LinkedIn. Includes score, date, and quiz name.
③ REVIEW ANSWERS
Taker sees each question with their answer highlighted as correct/wrong. Explanations shown if creator enabled them.
FAILED PATH
If retake is allowed, taker restarts from the intro screen. If not, a "final score" screen is shown with study recommendations.
CREATOR ANALYTICS
Score distribution histogram, per-question difficulty, average completion time, and individual response drill-down.
CREATOR SETTINGS
Creator controls whether answers are visible after submission, and whether a retake is permitted and how many times.
